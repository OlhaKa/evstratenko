Why Barcelona?

Every year more and more medical tourists from other countries are turning to Barcelona. Why?

Location

Wonderful location with finest and restful beaches, exelent food combined with rich cultural heritage. Plus direct and inexpensive trip from all the destinations. If you had the choice of a miserable winter spent recuperating or relaxing in the sun, which would you choose? Spain’s climate is sunny and warm for most of the year making a recuperation stay here a pleasant one.

Medical system

Spain offers excellent healthcare and has one of the most advanced medical systems in the world. The World Health Organisation(WHO) ranks the Spanish health service as the seventh best in the world while Newsweekmagazine puts it in third place behind Japan and Switzerland. Is one of the countries spending the most on private healthcare as a proportion of total healthcare, which is lot more than in France or Germany. And the private hospital sector accounts for 57% of the hospitals in Spain. Also dont forget about other important advantages like no waiting lists. Waiting periods for appointments in the most-used specialities are only just around 1-2 weeks

Clinics

Barcelona city has World-class hospitals and clinics, where treatments are provided by some of the best and highly trained physicians and surgeons. in the world. Medical institutions in Barcelona are equipped with new and advanced equipment, where research is constantly conducted, outstanding discoveries are made and new treatment technologies are being developed. And forget about those dull hospitals of your county with grey lights and an overbearing smell of bleach. In Barcelona hospitals the décor is as fancy as top-end hotel, and personal is friendly and carefull. It certainly helps to calm any anxieties patients may be feeling and makes for a far better experience for visiting loved ones.

Price

When it comes to your health and when you want to travel abroad for medical treatment. the price should never be your first concern. However, no one likes to waste money. Here you will find right balance of cost and quality! Medical costs in Spain are significantly lower (and often with higher standards and success rates) than in other europian countries. And it is certainly one of the main factors that keeps it so popular!!
